📊 Power BI Dataset Analysis Report
1. Dataset Overview
- Metrics Used:
- Sales (Sum of Sales)
- Profit (Sum of Profit)
- Trend Axis: City
- Target KPI: Profit compared against Sales
2. Key Insights
- Cities with high sales but low profit indicate inefficiency or high discounting.
- Cities with balanced sales and profit show strong business performance.
- Profit Margin % (Profit ÷ Sales) is a more meaningful KPI than raw profit, as it highlights efficiency.

3. Applied Filters
- Time Period: Year, Quarter, Month slicers for trend analysis.
- Product Category: Compare performance across categories (e.g., Furniture vs Electronics).
- Customer Segment: Consumer, Corporate, Home Office.
- Region/City: Drill-down from Region → City.
- Top N Filter: Focus on Top 5 or Top 10 cities by Sales/Profit.
- Profit Margin %: Highlight high vs low margin areas.
- Discount %: Understand impact of discounting on profitability.
4. Visual Recommendations
- KPI Card: Sales vs Profit with variance indicator.
- Ribbon Chart: Show city-wise ranking changes over time.
- Bar Chart: Compare categories or segments.
- Map Visual: Regional distribution of Sales and Profit.
- Slicers: Time, Category, Segment, Profit Margin for interactive filtering.
5. Actionable Insights
- Identify low-margin cities and investigate discounting or cost issues.
- Highlight top-performing categories for executive focus.
- Track seasonal trends with time slicers to optimize inventory and marketing.
- Use drill-through filters to deep dive into specific city or category performance.
